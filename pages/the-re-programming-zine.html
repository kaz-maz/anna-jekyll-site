The Re-Programming Zine, is a collection of comics and texts by Anahi Saravia Herrera, Anna Corfa Isehayek, Boe Huntress, Mio Ebisu, Ru-yenn Kwok and Sophie K Rosa. The project started as a response (or unofficial sequel) to the 2012 french zine, La Fabrication Artisanale des Comforts Affectifs.
